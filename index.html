Rasmalai

Ingredients:

  • Malai
  • Chenna
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Recepie:

  1. Get all the milk sauce ingredients together in a heavy bottom sauce pan.
  2. For the dumplings, bring the whole milk to a gentle boil in a heavy bottom non stick sauce pan.
  3. In the meanwhile, bring all the ingredients for the syrup together and bring to a boil.
  4. Remove them from the syrup and cool slightly.
